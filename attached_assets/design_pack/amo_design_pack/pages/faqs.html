What is silicone rendering?

Silicone rendering is a flexible, breathable and weather-resistant render finish often chosen for modern exterior wall finishes.

Is silicone render better than traditional render?

It depends on the property, existing surface and finish required. Silicone render is popular because it offers a clean finish and strong weather resistance.
